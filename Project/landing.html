About Myself

Thanks for visiting my Page! My name is Rajdeep. I completed my Masters in Analytics from University of Texas with specialization in Data Science and Big data.

Before coming for my masters I completed my Bachelors in Mechanical Engineering with specialization in Automotive design from Delhi College of Engineering. One of my all-time favorite quote by Dr. Steve Maraboli - "Stop letting your fear condemn you to mediocrity" defines my philosophical dream.

My educational background has enabled me to access tools and assess situations to go beyond the obvious. It gave extensive skills to break a problem into simple questions. My engineering skills lead to develop critical thinking and thrive for excellence.

I strongly believe in the potential of data driven products and currenly working in the SF Bay area to bring the data idea into reality.
I hope one fine day we embrace the potential which lies in data and bring the product into reality with right engineering and research skills.
